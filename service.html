Mental Health Training

_Certificate Programme._

More than ever, people are aware of the importance of talking about mental health and mental illness or disorder.
This is because of the realization that we all have mental health. It is just as important as physical health. Like physical health, mental health is a part of everyone’s life.

  • While physical health is the state of our body, mental health is the state of our mind, feelings, and emotions.
  • Of course, physical health can influence mental health, both positively and negatively; so can life experiences, work or school environment, relationships, and the community we live in.
  • While being mentally healthy contributes to feeling, thinking and acting in ways that help one enjoy life and cope with its challenges, struggling with mental health may result in feeling unhappy, difficulty thinking clearly, or feeling overwhelmed by stressful situations.

There's a lot to cover here.

FIRST AID & CPR

Accident is an inevitable reality of life. It will always happen. The most important thing is to be prepared before it happens and know what to do after it happens.

  • Imagine the helplessness, sadness and sorrow you will feel when you don’t know what to do to rescue an accident casualty in the event of an accident or the joy you will feel to rescue someone in a near death situation to be back to life.
  • Hence, the need for Cardio Pulmonary Resuscitation and Standard First Aid.
